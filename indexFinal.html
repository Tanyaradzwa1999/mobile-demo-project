What is web development?

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications that are accessible through the internet. It combines coding, design, and problem-solving skills to build sites that are not only functional but also user-friendly and visually appealing. There are two main parts to web development: front-end development, which focuses on what users see and interact with, and back-end development, which handles the behind-the-scenes operations like servers, databases, and application logic. This field is always evolving with new technologies and trends, allowing developers to come up with innovative solutions that meet the needs of both users and businesses.
