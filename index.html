About

I'm a passionate frontend developer with a strong foundation in electrical engineering and embedded systems. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, where I honed my skills in circuit design, microcontrollers, and embedded software. This technical background gives me a unique perspective when developing user interfaces, allowing me to bridge the gap between hardware and software seamlessly.

Over the years, I've transitioned into the world of web development, finding a love for creating intuitive and visually appealing interfaces. I have a keen eye for design and a knack for solving complex problems, which drives my approach to building responsive and user-friendly web applications. With experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React, I strive to deliver engaging digital experiences that resonate with users.

I'm always eager to learn and stay updated with the latest industry trends, continuously improving my craft. Whether it's collaborating with cross-functional teams or diving deep into new technologies, I thrive on challenges and am committed to delivering high-quality solutions.

What is web development ?

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites and web applications that are accessible through the internet. It combines coding, design, and problem-solving skills to build sites that are not only functional but also user-friendly and visually appealing. There are two main parts to web development: front-end development, which focuses on what users see and interact with, and back-end development, which handles the behind-the-scenes operations like servers, databases, and application logic. This field is always evolving with new technologies and trends, allowing developers to come up with innovative solutions that meet the needs of both users and businesses.
